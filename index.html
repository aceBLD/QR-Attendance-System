About QR Attendy

QR Attendy is an Web Application that both runs on its Official Website and the Application itself. The main purpose was to provide a quick, accurate, and dependable way to keep track of attendance. By streamlining the process for both students and teachers, it cuts down on manual tasks, minimizes human errors, and neatly organizes data in a secure digital format. Moreover, this system saves time, enhances record management, boosts transparency, and offers a more structured approach to monitoring attendance.
